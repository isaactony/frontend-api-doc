The **Call History and Analytics** serves as a vital tool for managing and analyzing your calls. Understanding these features can enhance your ability to filter, analyze, and optimize call outcomes, improving both operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

## Key Features

1. **Comprehensive Call Logging**: Every call is logged with detailed information including phone numbers, topics, sentiments, and outcomes
2. **Advanced Filtering**: Display only the calls relevant to your needs, such as those tagged for product support or specific outcomes
3. **Detailed Call Analysis**: Access conversation summaries, caller details, and call statistics
4. **Call Path Insights**: Understand how conversations flow and where they end for better routing optimization
5. **Recording Playback**: Listen to call recordings with adjustable speed and quality controls

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Call History" stepNumber={1}>
    Navigate to the Call History page from your Phonely Dashboard to view all logged calls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Filters" stepNumber={2}>
    Use the filtering options to display only calls relevant to your analysis needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Call Details" stepNumber={3}>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze Call Paths" stepNumber={4}>
    Review how conversations flow and identify where calls are ending to optimize your prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play Recordings" stepNumber={5}>

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Overview

Every call in Phonely is logged with comprehensive details such as phone numbers, topics, sentiments, and outcomes. These details allow you to filter and view only the relevant calls, focusing on specific aspects like product support or customer inquiries.

## Analyzing Call Details

The call history page provides a detailed view, including conversation summaries, caller details, and call statistics. This section also allows playback of call recordings and adjustment of playback features such as speed and quality.

## **Call Path Insights**
