## **Import an Existing Number**

If you already use a provider like Twilio or Vonage, you can connect those numbers directly to Phonely.

![Import Phone Numbers From Twillio Vonage Gi](/assets/import-phone-numbers-from-twillio-vonage.gif)

### **Import from Twilio**

1. Select the **Import Number** tab.
2. From the Import your number dropdown, choose From Twilio.
3. Enter your:
   - Twilio Account SID
   - Twilio Auth Token
4. Click **Import Number**.\
   Your Twilio number(s) will appear in the dashboard once verified.

### **Import from Vonage**

1. Under **Import your number**, select From Vonage.
2. Enter:
   - Phone Number
   - Vonage API Key
   - Vonage API Secret
3. Click **Import Number** to connect.

## **3. Port an Existing Number**

You can transfer (port) your current phone number to Phonely if you prefer to keep it.

### **Steps for Porting**

1. Select **Import Number** \> **Port Number**.
2. Review the porting steps shown:
   - **Fill out the form** â€“ Verify ownership by answering required questions.
   - **Wait for Porting** â€“ Phonely will request your number from the existing carrier.
   - **Use New Number** â€“ Porting completes within **14 days**.
3. Click **Process Verification** to open the port request form.

### **Port Request Form Requirements**

Fill in the **Number Port Request** form with details such as:

- Email address
- Address and billing details
- Landline or toll-free numbers (if applicable)
- Upload:
  - Letter of Authorization (LOA)
  - Most recent billing statement

<Danger>
  Do not cancel your existing carrier until Phonely confirms port completion.
</Danger>
