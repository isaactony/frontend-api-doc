### How to Enable

1. Locate **Humanize Conversation** in the Voice & Personality settings.
2. Toggle the switch on to enable it.
3. Your agent will begin incorporating natural speech patterns during conversations.

<Tip>

</Tip>

## Phonetic Dictionary

The Phonetic Dictionary allows you to fine-tune pronunciation for specific words, names, or brands ensuring your agent says them correctly and naturally.

This is especially helpful for:

- **Company names** (e.g., ‚ÄúPhonely‚Äù pronounced as ‚ÄúFone-lee‚Äù).
- **Acronyms** (e.g., ‚ÄúMFA‚Äù pronounced ‚ÄúEm-Eff-Ay‚Äù).
- Foreign or uncommon names.

### How It Works

You define how a word should be pronounced using phonemes (sound-based syllables).   \
Phonely supports standard phonetic notation systems, such as MFA (Montreal Forced Aligner).

### How to Add a New Word

1. Scroll to the **Phonetic Dictionary** section.
2. In the **Word** field, type the term you want to adjust.
3. In the **Phonemes** field, enter the pronunciation.
4. Click **\+ Add Word** to save it.
