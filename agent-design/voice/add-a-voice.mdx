The **Add Voice** feature lets you import or connect external voices (for example, from 11 Labs) into your Phonely Voice Library. You can then use these imported voices in your agents and workflows.

![Add Voice 11 Labs Gi](/assets/add-voice-11-labs.gif)

## **How to Add a New Voice**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Voice Library" titleSize="h3">
    1. Go to the **Voices** section of your Phonely dashboard.
    2. You’ll see:
       - **Featured Voices** — curated, ready-to-use AI voices.
       - **Explore All Voices** — all available voices, including imported and cloned ones.
    3. Click the **\+ Add Voice** button on the upper right or beneath the “Explore All Voices” section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Provider" titleSize="h3">
    1. In the **Add a Voice** window, open the **Provider** dropdown.
    2. Select your preferred provider, such as **11 Labs**.
       - Phonely currently supports third-party providers like _11 Labs_ for realistic and multilingual voice options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the Voice ID" titleSize="h3">
    1. After selecting the provider, you’ll see an **Add ID** field.
    2. Paste your **voice ID** from the provider’s platform (e.g., from your 11 Labs account).
       - This ID uniquely identifies the voice you want to import.
    3. Double-check that the ID is correct to ensure the voice loads successfully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Language">
    1. Choose the **Language** in which the voice will operate.
    2. Available options may include: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Hindi, Dutch, Swedish, and Danish
    3. Select the language that best matches your voice model or intended audience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and Add the Voice" titleSize="h3">
    1. Review your selections (Provider, ID, Language).
    2. Click **\+ Add Voice** to finalize the process.
    3. The new voice will now appear in your Voice Library under _Explore All Voices_.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage and Use the New Voice" titleSize="h3">
    Once added, you can:

    - **Preview** the voice by clicking the play button.
    - **Set it as default** by clicking **Select**.
    - **Add to Favorites** by tapping the heart icon ❤️.
    - **Edit settings** (e.g., tone, speed, or pitch) using the gear icon ⚙️ if available.
  </Step>
</Steps>
