## What is Phonely?

Phonely is the easiest way to build conversational voice agents that answer and place phone calls. You get the full conversation stack out-of-the-box voice, reasoning, routing, integrations, analytics so you can go from idea to a production agent in minutes, not months.

With Phonely you can:

- Have natural, low-latency phone conversations
- Make and receive calls on Phonely numbers or your carrier
- Qualify, route, schedule, and escalate to humans on rules you define
- Integrate with calendars, CRMs, APIs, and internal tools
- Track performance with rich call analytics, A/B testing, and call paths

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Do I need to know how to code?">
  Nope! Phonely is fully **no-code**. You can design, test, and deploy an AI phone agent directly in the **Flow Builder** using simple prompts and drag-and-drop blocks.

  For advanced users, we support **custom APIs**, **webhooks**, and **cURL imports** for deeper integrations.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can Phonely integrate with my CRM or scheduling software?">
  Yes — Phonely integrates with **Google Calendar**, **HubSpot**, **Salesforce**, **Zapier**, and any system that supports REST APIs.\
  You can pass variables like `@name`, `@dob`, or `@appointment_time` into live requests and automate actions in real time.

  <Info>
    **Pro Tip:**\
    If Phonely doesn’t natively connect to your software, you can **import any integration using a cURL request** directly into the **API Request block**.
  </Info>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can Phonely replace my existing call center?">
  In many cases, yes.\
  Phonely agents are already **matching or outperforming human call center agents** at **up to 70% cost reduction** — while handling calls 24/7, in any language.

  For higher call volumes (2,000+ per month), we offer **white-glove onboarding** and dedicated engineering support.\
  [Talk to Sales →](https://forms.phonely.ai/t/hoGHsk9hDzus)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I train my AI agent?">
  Training an agent is as simple as uploading your **knowledge base** (FAQs, SOPs, or website) and defining your core **flows** — the logical paths your calls follow.

  Each flow can include talk blocks, integrations, variables, and post-call actions.\
  Phonely’s AI assists with writing prompts, generating responses, and connecting APIs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can Phonely make outbound calls?">
  Yes. Phonely supports **outbound campaigns** for reminders, reactivation, or follow-ups.\
  You can upload a CSV, connect a Google Sheet, or use an API to send call data.

  It also includes:

  - **Voicemail detection**
  - **Retry logic** (e.g. redial after 30 minutes)
  - **Custom retry messages**
  - **Compliance tools** for DNC and consent tracking
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What analytics does Phonely provide?">
  Phonely’s analytics dashboard includes:

  - **Call summaries** (AI-generated)
  - **Sentiment analysis**
  - **Topic clustering**
  - **A/B testing results**
  - **Call path visualization**
  - **Performance metrics** (CSAT proxy, conversion, containment rate)

  Learn more on our [Analytics page](/capabilities/analytics).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can Phonely speak multiple languages?">
  Yes. Phonely supports multilingual voice agents using top-tier speech models and cloned voices.\
  You can configure voices for **English**, **Spanish**, **French**, and many others — including **regional accents** and **custom voice cloning** for your brand.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Phonely SOC, HIPPA or GDPR compliant?">
  Absolutely.\
  Phonely is built with enterprise-grade security and supports compliance frameworks like **SOC 2**, **HIPAA**, and **GDPR**.

  Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and integrations use secure API tokens or OAuth.
</Accordion>
